Our providers

PushFeedback relies on trusted third-party subprocessors to deliver reliable services. All subprocessors comply with Safe Harbor regulations and applicable data protection laws in the US and Europe. These subprocessors help us provide various aspects of our service, from hosting to infrastructure management.

Brevo

Manages email services.

Location: EU

Cloudflare

Provides secure and scalable storage.

Location: USA

GitHub

Hosts our code.

Location: USA

DigitalOcean

Provides hosting and infrastructure.

Location: USA and EU

Sentry

Monitors errors.

Location: USA

Stripe

Handles secure payments.

Location: USA

Netlify

Hosts our web content.

Location: USA

Optional integrations​

These are optional third-party services that you can choose to integrate with PushFeedback. Data is only shared with these providers if you explicitly configure the integration.

Zapier

Automates workflows by connecting with thousands of other apps.

Location: USA

Make

Enables advanced automation scenarios and complex integrations.

Location: USA

Microsoft Teams

Sends feedback notifications to your Teams channels.

Location: USA

Slack

Delivers feedback notifications to your Slack workspace.

Location: USA
